User Manual: Fitness Tracker Application

Welcome to the Fitness Tracker Application, fitness.py! This application helps you keep track of your workouts and personal fitness goals. Below is a guide on how to use the different features of the application.

1. Main Interface:

Upon launching the application, you will see the main interface consisting of different pages accessible through the navigation bar at the top. The available pages are:

Dashboard: Provides an overview of your fitness progress, as in the most recently tracked exercise.
Workout Log: Interface for logging your different workouts. 
Personal Goals: Interface for tracking and logging your personal goals.

2. Navigation Bar:

The navigation bar at the top of the window contains buttons for each page of the application. Clicking on a button will navigate you to the corresponding page.

7. Exiting the Application:

To exit the application, click on the "Exit" button located on the Dashboard page. This will close the application window.

That's it! You are now ready to use fitness.py to monitor your fitness progress and achieve your personal fitness goals. Thank you for reading.
